Breaking Barriers: Understanding Mental Health Among Youth

"Shattering Stigmas: Exploring Mental Health Struggles Among Youth"

  • Projects
  • / Breaking Barriers: Understanding Mental Health Among Youth

By Editorial Team

11 December 2024

Mental health is a crucial aspect of our well-being, yet it often remains a topic shrouded in stigma and misunderstanding. Recognizing the need for open conversations and awareness, Rotaract Club of UCBS launched the Breaking Barriers series, with its first session focusing on "Understanding Depression and Anxiety Issues Among Youth."

On December 11, 2024, at 6:00 PM, students, faculty, and mental health advocates gathered virtually on Google Meet to hear from Dr. Ravi Sharma (MD-Psychiatry). His insightful session shed light on the signs of mental health struggles, practical strategies to cope with stress and anxiety, and the importance of seeking help when needed.

Fostering Awareness and Building Resilience

Dr. Sharma emphasized that mental health issues are more common than we realize and that acknowledging them is the first step toward healing. The session covered essential topics, including:
✔️ Recognizing the early signs of anxiety and depression.
✔️ The impact of social and academic pressures on youth mental health.
✔️ Practical techniques for managing stress and building emotional resilience.
✔️ Breaking the stigma around seeking professional help.

 🔗 Join the Breaking Barriers WhatsApp Group

The discussion was interactive, allowing participants to voice their concerns and ask questions, creating a safe and supportive space for dialogue.

A Community of Support and Understanding

The event also marked the beginning of an initiative aimed at encouraging open conversations about mental health. To ensure continuous engagement and provide access to resources, a WhatsApp group has been created, where members can stay updated on future sessions and discuss various aspects of mental well-being.

Looking Ahead: Your Voice Matters!

As we continue the Breaking Barriers series, we want to hear from you! What mental health topics would you like us to cover in future sessions? Your input will help shape meaningful discussions that benefit our entire community.
